About Program

Established in 2015 as one of the Ministry of Human Resource Development's third-generation IIMs, IIM Sirmaur upholds the IIM legacy by offering quality education, training, and management research. We focus on nurturing indigenous, erudite, and innovative practices to empower our students. IIM Sirmaur is delighted to present "Sierra," a prestigious Management, Sports, and Cultural Fest taking place in the end of January 2025. This vibrant event will attract talented minds from esteemed institutes nationwide, engaging them in a wide array of activities. Sierra has become a local highlight, drawing participants from over 500 top colleges across the country.
